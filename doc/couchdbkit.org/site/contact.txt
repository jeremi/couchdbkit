title: Getting help with Couchdbiit

There is a mailing-list for now on "Google groups":http://groups.google.com/group/couchdbkit where you can follow changes and ask questions about Couchdbkit. You can also chat with users and developers on "#couchdbkit irc channel":irc://irc.freenode.net/couchdbkit or send a ticket on "bitbucket":http://bitbucket.org/benoitc/couchdbkit/.

The best way to get help with Couchdbkit is to email me directly. You can do it on "benoitc@e-engura.org":mailto:benoitc@e-engura.org .
